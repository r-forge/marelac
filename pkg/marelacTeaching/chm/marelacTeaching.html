marelacTeaching-package(marelacTeaching)R Documentation

Datasets and tutorials for use in the MArine Riverine Estuarine LAcustrine and Coastal sciences

Description

R-package marelacTeaching has been designed as a tool to help in teaching students from the MArine, Riverine, Estuarine, LAcustrine and Coastal sciences.

It contains:

  • a tutorial for novice R-users, focussing on the use of R in scientific computing.
  • data sets used in science teaching.
